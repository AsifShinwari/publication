International Conference on Computer, Communication and Signal Processing

The proposed conference, ICCCSP (International Conference on Computer, Communication, and Signal Processing) 2021 - is an event that sheds the spotlight on the recent trends in the research areas of Computer Science, Communications, and Signal Processing. The 2021 edition of the annual conference holds its lens on leveraging AI techniques for Healthcare. AI in Healthcare is an interdisciplinary, dynamic field with significant relevance to the current situation. This event brings together researchers, students, and practitioners who are inclined towards health care research and applications. Research techniques may include Big Data Analytics, Data Engineering, Data Mining, Statistical Learning and Modelling, Machine Learning, Visualization, Predictive Analytics, ensuring Security & Privacy, and more. Attend the AI in Healthcare conference to hear how leading healthcare researchers and organizations have leveraged AI's power to improve patient care and ROI.
